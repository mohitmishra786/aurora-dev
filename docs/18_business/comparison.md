# Comparison

Us vs Them.

**Last Updated:** February 8, 2026
**Audience:** Buyers

## Aurora vs Copilot

- **Copilot:** Autocomplete. Helpful, but passive.
- **Aurora:** Autonomous. You assign a task, it does the work.

## Aurora vs Devin

- **Devin:** Closed source. Expensive. Cloud only.
- **Aurora:** Open Source. Self-Hostable. Extensible.

## Aurora vs Custom Scripts

- **Scripts:** Fragile. Hard to maintain.
- **Aurora:** Robust, observable, and learns from mistakes.
