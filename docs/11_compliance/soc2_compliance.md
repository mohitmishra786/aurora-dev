## Controls

We adhere to the Trust Services Criteria:
1. **Security:** Firewalls, MFA, encryption.
2. **Availability:** DR plans, backups.
3. **Confidentiality:** Data classification, NDA.
4. **Processing Integrity:** Tests, validation.
5. **Privacy:** GDPR alignment.

## Evidence

We use **Vanta** to automatically collect evidence.
- Agent installed on all laptops.
- CloudWatch logs integration.
- GitHub access reviews.

## Audit Period

Our observation window is Jan 1 - Dec 31.
Reports are issued annually by our external auditor.
