## Community Support

- **Discord:** Chat with other users.
- **GitHub Issues:** Report bugs.
- **StackOverflow:** Tag `aurora-dev`.

## Commercial Support

- **Standard:** Email support (24h SLA).
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated Slack channel + Zoom calls (1h SLA).

## Information to Provide

When asking for help, always valid:
1. `aurora version`
2. Logs (`aurora logs --tail 50`)
3. A reproduction script.
